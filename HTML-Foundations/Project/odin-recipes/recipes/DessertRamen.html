Dessert Ramen

This is a recipe for dessert ramen I made with friends. Tastes like cough syrup.

Important: Ingredients must be purchased at a Dollar Tree or similarly cheap location for expected subpar results.

Ingredients:
  • Twizzlers Ropes
  • Unicorn Ice Cream
  • Fruit Tic Tacs
  • Fruit Punch Soda
  • Swedish Fish
Instructions:
  1. Pull apart the Twizzlers into thin individual ropes.
  2. Place ropes and a scoop of unicorn ice cream into a bowl
  3. Pour in fruit punch soda
  4. Garnish with tic tacs and swedish fish
  5. Enjoy?
